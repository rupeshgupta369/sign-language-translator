Text to Sign Language (ASL) Converter

About

American Sign Language (ASL) is a natural language. It is the primary sign language used by the deaf and people with hearing impairment in the USA and Canada. This WeCapable Tool easily converts English text into sign language symbols. This tool will be very useful for both teaching and learning American sign language.
